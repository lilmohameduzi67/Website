Who are the best players?

Soccer has many great players. Some of these players you may have heard of include messi and ronaldo who are considered to be the best players of our generation. Pele has accomplished the impossible and has won 3 world cups for Brazil. Pele is the only person to have won 3 world cups while being best player of the match multiple times. Soccer fanatics belive that Brazil would not have won 3 world cups without the help of pele. Pele also has many other accomplichments, including having scored 1000+ goals and winning best player on the planet multiple times.

Finally, The biggest and most sought after trophy, the world cup. This trophy is handed out to the best international teams and the competion is only held every 4 years.
